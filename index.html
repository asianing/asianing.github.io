About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Texas at San Antonio, pursuing an Aerospace Certificate. My work lives at the crossroads of precision engineering and creative expression.

From designing 3D-printed functional prototypes to scripting immersive Roblox environments in Luau — I build things end to end. I care deeply about how objects feel, move, and exist in space.

Currently developing EcoGenZ, an environmental education game, and expanding my Blender + CAD portfolio toward aerospace applications.
